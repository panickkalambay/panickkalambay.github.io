Teaching Philosophy

I believe effective engineering education bridges rigorous theory with real-world problem-solving. In my teaching. I apply the KEEN 3C approach (Curiosity, Connections, and Creating Value) to encourage students to ask thoughtful questions, link engineering concepts across disciplines and real-world contexts, and develop solutions that create literacy, and the application of quantitative methods to practical transportation and infrastructure challenges. I am committed to fostering an inclusive learning environment where students feel supported in developing both technical competence and professional communication skills.
